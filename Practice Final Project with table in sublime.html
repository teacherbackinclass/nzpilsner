Recipe Fermentable Hops Yeast

Recipe

Fermentables( 11lbs 12oz)

  • 6lbs Pale Ale Malt 2-Row
  • 5lbs Pilsner Malt
  • 6 oz Rolled Oats
  • 6 oz Wheat
  • Pre-boil Gravity 1.048
  • Orignial Gravity 1.053
  • Color 4.8 SRM

Hops (1.75oz)

Weight Type Time IBU amount
0.25oz Motueka (6.2% AA) First Wort Addition ~6.1 IBU
0.5oz Nelson Sauvin (12% AA) Last 10 Minutes of Boil ~7.5 IBU
1 oz Wakatu (7.5% AA) Last 5 Minutes of Boil ~7.5 IBU

Yeast
